## Ukemi（Breakfall)

### Ushiro Ukemi

1. 顎を強く引き、後頭部を畳につけない (Tuck the chin and keep the back of the head off the mat)
2. 両腕で斜め45度に強く畳を叩く (Slap the mat at a 45-degree angle with both arms)
3. 足を上げて衝撃を背中全体に分散する (Lift the legs to spread the impact across the back)

### Yoko Ukemi

1. 横向きに丸く落ちて肩から接地する (Land in a rounded position starting from the shoulder)
2. 下側の腕で強く受け身を叩く (Use the bottom arm to slap the mat strongly)
3. 首を浮かせて頭を畳に打たない (Keep the head off the mat)

### Mae Mawari Ukemi

1. 肩から背中へ円を描くように回転する (Roll in a circular motion from shoulder to back)
2. 肘を伸ばしたまま突っ込まない (Do not dive with straight arms)
3. 立ち上がりまで流れるようにつなげる (Flow smoothly back to standing)

## Solo Newaza Workout

### Soto Mawashi (Outside Legs Rotation)

1. 股関節から大きく回す (Rotate widely from the hips)
2. 膝ではなく足全体をコントロールする (Control the entire leg, not just the knee)
3. 腰を浮かせず腹圧を保つ (Keep the hips down and maintain core pressure)

### Uchi Mawashi (Inside Legs Rotation)

1. 内転筋を意識して回転する (Rotate using the inner thigh muscles)
2. 足先だけでなく膝の向きも揃える (Align the knees as well as the feet)
3. 動作を速くしすぎず可動域を優先する (Prioritize range of motion over speed)

### Ashi Keri (Kicking the Legs)

1. 下腹部を締めて蹴る (Kick while tightening the lower abdomen)
2. 膝を伸ばし切らず連続動作する (Keep continuous motion without fully locking the knees)
3. 寝技での距離管理を意識する (Focus on distance management for groundwork)

### Ashi Furi (Swing Legs)

1. 骨盤から脚を振る (Swing the legs from the pelvis)
2. 上半身を固めてバランスを維持する (Stabilize the upper body to maintain balance)
3. ガードの切り替えを意識する (Focus on transitioning between guards)

### Ashi Kosa (Cross Legs)

1. 足を交差させても腰を浮かせない (Do not lift the hips while crossing the legs)
2. 素早く左右を切り替える (Switch sides quickly)
3. 相手を挟む感覚を意識する (Develop the feeling of trapping the opponent)

## OSAEKOMI (Pinning)

### Kesa Gatame Basic

1. 相手の頭と腕を密着して固定する (Secure the opponent’s head and arm tightly)
2. 胸を相手に被せて腰を浮かせない (Apply chest pressure without lifting the hips)
3. 足を広げて重心を安定させる (Spread the legs for stable balance)

### Yoko Shiho Gatame Basic

1. 相手の肩と腰を四方向で抑える (Control the opponent’s shoulders and hips from four directions)
2. 胸圧をかけ続ける (Maintain continuous chest pressure)
3. 相手のエビを先に潰す (Shut down the opponent’s shrimp escape first)

### Kata Gatame Basic

1. 相手の腕を自分の首に密着させる (Trap the opponent’s arm tightly against your neck)
2. 頭を低くして肩圧をかける (Keep the head low and apply shoulder pressure)
3. 隙間を作らず胸を密着する (Keep chest contact without leaving space)

### Kami Shiho Gatame

1. 相手の肩を両腕で包み込む (Wrap both arms around the opponent’s shoulders)
2. 頭側に重心を置く (Keep your weight toward the head side)
3. 相手の腕を自由にさせない (Do not allow the opponent’s arms to move freely)

### Around the World (15 Osaekomi Transitions)

1. 常に胸圧を切らさない (Never lose chest pressure)
2. 相手の逃げ方向に先回りする (Move ahead of the opponent’s escape direction)
3. 移動中も膝を浮かせすぎない (Do not raise the knees too much while moving)

## KUMIKATA in AI YOTSU

### Win Kumikata in Ai Yotsu (same side grips)

1. 先に釣り手を作る (Establish the collar hand first)
2. 相手の袖を殺して主導権を握る (Control the opponent’s sleeve to take initiative)
3. 足を止めず動きながら組む (Grip while constantly moving your feet)

### Simple Gripping in Ai Yotsu No.1 - No.3

1. 相手の肘の向きを崩す (Disrupt the direction of the opponent’s elbow)
2. 組み負けても姿勢を崩さない (Maintain posture even if losing the grip exchange)
3. 自分の得意技につながる位置を作る (Create positions leading to your favorite techniques)

### Simple Gripping in Ai Yotsu No.3 - No.4

1. 相手の釣り手を制限する (Limit the opponent’s collar hand)
2. 引き手で相手の肩線を操作する (Control the opponent’s shoulder line with the sleeve hand)
3. 一瞬で深く持つ (Secure a deep grip instantly)

### Get Sleeve in Kenka Yotsu No.1 - No.2

1. 相手の引き手側を先に制する (Control the opponent’s sleeve side first)
2. 距離を遠くしすぎない (Do not create too much distance)
3. 袖を持った瞬間に引く (Pull immediately once you secure the sleeve)

### Get Sleeve in Kenka Yotsu No.3 - No.4

1. 相手の袖口ではなく肘付近を意識する (Focus near the elbow rather than the sleeve end)
2. 頭を相手の中心線から外す (Keep your head off the opponent’s center line)
3. 組みながら前進圧力をかける (Apply forward pressure while gripping)

## KUMIKATA in KENKA YOTSU

### Win Kumikata in Kenka Yotsu (opposite side grips)

1. 相手の奥襟を許さない (Do not allow a deep collar grip)
2. 頭の位置で優位を取る (Gain advantage with head position)
3. 足をクロスさせない (Do not cross your feet)

### Simple Gripping in Kenka Yotsu No.1 No.2 & No.2 Advance

1. 先に袖を確保する (Secure the sleeve first)
2. 相手の肩を開かせる (Force the opponent’s shoulders open)
3. 投げへの連携を止めない (Keep the transition to throws continuous)

### Simple Gripping in Kenka Yotsu No.3 No.4

1. 相手の釣り手側に圧力をかける (Pressure the opponent’s collar-hand side)
2. 横移動を使う (Use lateral movement)
3. 一度切ってから入り直す (Break the grip once and re-enter)

### Simple Gripping in Ai Yotsu No.5 No.6

1. 相手の肘を伸ばさせる (Force the opponent’s elbow to extend)
2. 自分の姿勢を高く保つ (Maintain a tall posture)
3. 片手の攻防で終わらせない (Do not rely on one-handed exchanges only)

### Simple Gripping in Ai Yotsu No.7

1. 相手の重心移動を読む (Read the opponent’s weight shifts)
2. 手だけでなく身体全体で組む (Grip using the whole body, not just the hands)
3. 組んだ直後に攻撃姿勢へ入る (Enter an attacking stance immediately after gripping)

## Break the Gripping in AI YOTSU

### Breaking the Sleeve in Ai Yotsu

1. 相手の親指方向へ切る (Break toward the opponent’s thumb direction)
2. 肘を曲げて力を逃がさない (Keep the elbow bent to maintain power)
3. 足も同時に動かして切る (Move the feet while breaking the grip)

### Breaking the Collar in Ai Yotsu No.1

1. 下から上に弾く (Pop the grip upward from below)
2. 頭を外側へ逃がす (Move the head to the outside)
3. 切った直後に組み返す (Re-grip immediately after breaking)

### Breaking the Collar in Ai Yotsu No.2

1. 両手を連動させる (Use both hands together)
2. 相手の力が弱い瞬間を狙う (Attack the moment the opponent’s grip weakens)
3. 後ろへ下がりながら切らない (Do not retreat while breaking the grip)

## Break the Gripping in KENKA YOTSU

### Breaking the Sleeve in Kenka Yotsu No.1 - No.3

1. 相手の肘方向へ力を逃がす (Redirect force toward the opponent’s elbow)
2. 引きながら切る (Break the grip while pulling)
3. 切った後に頭位置を取る (Establish head position after breaking)

### Breaking the Collar in Kenka Yotsu

1. 相手の奥襟を深くさせない (Do not allow a deep back collar grip)
2. 肩を回転させて外す (Rotate the shoulder to remove the grip)
3. 防御だけで終わらず攻撃へつなぐ (Transition from defense into attack)

### Avoid His Tsuri Te (collar hand) in Kenka Yotsu

1. 相手の釣り手側へ頭を置かない (Do not place your head on the opponent’s collar-hand side)
2. 手首を払って距離を取る (Parry the wrist and create distance)
3. 相手に先に深く持たせない (Do not allow a deep grip first)

## KANSETSU WAZA (Arm Lock)

### JUJI GATAME Basic

1. 膝を締めて腕を固定する (Pinch the knees tightly to secure the arm)
2. 親指を上に向けて極める (Finish with the thumb pointing upward)
3. 腰を浮かせて一気に伸ばす (Lift the hips and extend explosively)

### UDE GATAME No.1 (When he gets the pants)

1. 相手の肘を身体に密着する (Keep the opponent’s elbow tight to your body)
2. 手首だけでなく肩も固定する (Control the shoulder as well as the wrist)
3. 相手が逃げる方向へ圧をかける (Apply pressure toward the opponent’s escape direction)

### Waki Gatame Variations

1. 脇で肘をしっかり挟む (Clamp the elbow tightly with the armpit)
2. 自分の体重を使って極める (Use your body weight to apply the lock)
3. 急激に極めすぎない (Do not apply the lock too suddenly)

### HIZA GATAME with KOMUlock in Butterfly Guard

1. 膝で肘を固定する (Control the elbow using the knee)
2. 上体を起こして圧を維持する (Keep the upper body upright to maintain pressure)
3. 相手の回転方向を止める (Stop the opponent’s rotational movement)

### KESA GATAME Armlock No.1 (ASHI GATAME)

1. 相手の腕を脚で挟む (Trap the opponent’s arm with the legs)
2. 胸圧を切らさない (Maintain chest pressure at all times)
3. 腰を引いて伸ばす (Pull the hips back to extend the arm)

## SHIME (Choking Techniques)

1. 前腕を首に深く入れる (Insert the forearm deeply around the neck)
2. 背中に密着して逃がさない (Stay tight to the opponent’s back)

### OKURI ERI JIME

2. 引き手で首を送る (Guide the neck using the pulling hand)
3. 胸を密着して空間を消す (Eliminate space with chest pressure)

### OKURI ERI JIME Escape No.1

2. 相手の肘を下げる (Pull down the opponent’s elbow)

### KATA HA JIME

2. 頭を低くして圧をかける (Keep the head low and apply pressure)
3. 首だけでなく肩も締める (Compress both the neck and shoulder)

### JUJI GATAME → SANKAKU

2. 足を素早く組み替える (Reposition the legs quickly)
